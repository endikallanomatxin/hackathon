## Judging criteria (no sé si aplicará)

The judging panel will evaluate each team based on a combination of factors,
including the completeness of submitted materials, project innovation,
difficulty level, and audience voting. Based on the final scores, the 1st, 2nd,
and 3rd place winning teams will be selected. All other teams will receive
special event souvenirs from the organizer. 

All participants should follow the requirements below to begin project
development:
- Projects must use the NVIDIA Jetson Platform to complete edge deployment. 
- Development must be based on LeRobot.
- Only SO-ARM 101 and Fashion Start Arm are allowed for use during the
competition.
- Each team will have 10 minutes for a live demo and presentation.
